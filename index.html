About Me

I'm Alec. I grew up in Austin and Round Rock, swimming since 2005 and taking apart anything I could get my hands on. Both stuck. The pool taught me that the best part of any of it is the people you do it with. The workbench taught me how good it feels to make the thing yourself.

These days I work in biotech, where the details matter and getting them right is most of the job. Outside of work, I'm usually mid project, building the things I wish someone else had already made. A little electronics, a little woodwork, mostly things I'd rather make than buy.

This site is where I'm logging most of it.

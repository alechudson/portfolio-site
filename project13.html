Keychain

About This Project

A tiny just-for-fun keychain that ended up being a nice excuse to mix a few processes. The brass piece was machined first, then fitted into a CNC-cut acrylic frame. A 3D printed shell closes around it with magnets, so the whole thing packs up neatly when it is not being shown off.
